About us

our story

When I opened Balance & Power Fitness Studio in 2018, I wanted to create a space where fitness transforms both body and mind. After recovering from a back injury through a combination of pilates, yoga, and kickboxing, I discovered the power of integrating these disciplines. What started as a small studio with just five mats has grown into a wellness center serving our community. Though we've expanded, our philosophy remains: movement is medicine. We meet each client where they are, believing every class is an opportunity to help someone discover their strength and find joy in movement.

our mission

At Balance & Power Fitness Studio, we create an inclusive community where fitness nurtures both body and mind. Through our pilates, yoga, and kickboxing programs, we build core strength, develop mindfulness, foster confidence, and create a supportive environment. We focus on proper technique while challenging each student to explore their capabilities. Success for us means clients feeling more energized, confident, and balanced in their daily lives, whether they're recovering from injury, seeking stress relief, or improving athletic performance.
